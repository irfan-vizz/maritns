Expanded Content Labels, also known as Extended Content Labels (ECL) are used to increase the amount of space available for text and graphics to provide consumers with more information.

When packaging products are small and label space is limited, Extended Content Labels (ECLs) provide the necessary space for additional graphics and copy through the use of multiple pages.

Extended Content Labels (ECLs) are commonly found on Health and Beauty, Pharmaceutical, Vape and Cannabis products where packaging profiles are small and it is difficult to find space for government mandated health and safety information.
